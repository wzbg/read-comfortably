Playing Cards

Decks are standard poker sized playing cards printed on high quality 310 GSM Linen Stock. There are no repeat pictures in the decks - that's 54 different ice pictures and 54 different fire pictures (plus the deck back artwork!). Decks are printed by MPC and come in full color tuck boxes.

All decks come with a matching sticker sheet of postage stamp sized stickers (can be used for deck seals or whatever you want) and pins/magnets (these are mid-sized round pins with a magnet you can place on the back so they work as a button or magnet).

Books

Ice Dragons & Other Rare Arctic Creatures is a 6x9 inch (hardcover or paperback) 100 page field guide. The book begins with Ice Dragons, but then moves on to cover all sorts of ice creatures including yeti, ice fae, frost giants, trolls, and much more!

Also available is Fire Dragons & Other Rare Ember Creatures. This is a matching 100 page field guide covering fire dragons, phoenixes and other fire creatures.

All books come with matching bookmarks and 4x6 prints.

Egg Replicas

Each egg starts with a wooden egg that is then textured via paper sculpture to look like a dragon egg, hand painted, and varnished. EACH IS UNIQUE! Eggs are lightweight and durable. You can choose 2" or 3" Frost or Flame eggs. Eggs come with a certificate signed by the artist.

50x60 Fabric Prints

I'm SO excited to have a stretch goal for HUGE 50" x 60" Wall Tapestry fabric prints! These are prints ON fabric great for indoor OR outdoor use. About the Paintings:

The original paintings offered as the kickstarter rewards are watercolor and may also include some pen & ink work (see below for examples). These are FROM the book/deck. Backers will choose their paintings in the order they pledged, but there are always a few extra so backers will always have multiple options.

2016 Pricing & Shipping changes:

Due to increased printing costs for books AND a big shipping cost increase on the part of the US Postal Service I've had to adjust my projects a bit for 2016 compared to those run in 2015. To make sure backers still get a lot for their money I've made reward levels include some lightweight extra stuff including bookmarks and 4x6 prints with all books, and sticker sheets/pins with all the decks. These don't add much to shipping costs, so hopefully the balance out some of the increases. In addition the egg replicas are fairly low priced for hand made items and are made to be lightweight and cheaper to ship. It matters a LOT to me to provide quality products and make sure backers are happy with what they get. I have no control on shipping and import costs and do fairly small bulk runs (300 or less of each deck and about 200 of each book), but I try to do everything I can to get you awesome rewards at the best prices possible. Thank you! :)
