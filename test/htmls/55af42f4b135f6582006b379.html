From High Fashion To Instagram - Tilda Lindstam May Be The Funniest Model On Social Media

Published on: July 21, 2015

"I'm pretty sure there's a lot more to life than being really, really ridiculously good looking." - Derek Zoolander

In the high fashion world people take themselves way too seriously. It's their job to look fantastic at all times, it's an ultra competitive field, and you can lose your standing at any point without notice. There's always a new 'IT girl,' there's always a new, hot designer. It's a tough business to get in, and some models give everything they have to be a part of it. Then you have models like Tilda Lindstam who seems to be able to make a joke out of everything.

The Swedish runway and print model has walked for the heavyweights of the industry. Valentino, Dior, Chloé, Dolce & Gabbana, and Givenchy. She's been featured in countless magazines like Vogue and Glamour. She may even make an appearance in the upcoming Zoolander sequel (no word on that, although she did walk with Ben Stiller and Owen Wilson during their promo run at fashion week). She may have accomplishments most models would kill for, but she still isn't taking herself seriously.

Her Instagram page continues to gain followers who appreciate her brand of humor. It's cool to see a high fashion model act so goofy and clown all the time. Whether she's taking selfies with unsuspecting passengers on her flights or sprawling out next to a stack of Cheez-It boxes in Costco, she's got no shame. It just goes to show that no matter your position in life you should always be laughing. Life's too short to be uptight.
