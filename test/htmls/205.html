Fantastic 3D paper art jungle bursts with colours and life

24th July by Tilly Martin in Art

  • Tweet

Following on from Mlle Hipolyte’s unbelievable 3D animal masks, the talented French illustrator and paper designer created a tropical jungle scene. Using tiny pieces of coloured paper cut entirely by hand, the vibrant artwork replicates the lush rainforest environment. Bursting with botanical life, tropical birds and wild creatures peeping through the leaves, the 3D composition is stunning. Taking about six hours to assemble to the wall, the fantastic paper jungle is an inspiring addition to the growing paper art trend. View more of the designer's work at hipolyte.com.

Via Design Boom
