Caledonia Silva Kiloran Coffee Table

The table.

  • Story: Sara Harowitz

Inspired by designer Oliver Scott’s childhood summers in Scotland, Victoria-based Caledonia Silva’s Bothy collection celebrates the natural beauty and warmth of wood. The Kiloran Coffee Table, named after Kiloran Bay on the Isle of Colonsay, offers an invigorated take on an often disregarded home furnishing. The top of the table is mitered—meaning it is put together without the use of metal fasteners or screws—giving it a smooth, seamless appearance. With open cubbies for storing books and games, the Kiloran’s interesting lines and clean shape return the coffee table to its rightful place as the focal point of the living room.
