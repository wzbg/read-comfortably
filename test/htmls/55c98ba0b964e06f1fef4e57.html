Nike Just Made Its "Wheat" Sneakers Even Better (UPDATE)

By Rajah Allarey, Riley Jones
0

UPDATE (8/10/15, 1:30 p.m.):

Sneakers.fr has dropped off official images of the Nike Sportswear 2015 "Wheat Pack." In addition to the sneakers previewed last June, there will also be an Air Tech Challenge 3. Per Sneakers.fr, this collection will hit the Nike SNKRS app in November with prices ranging from $130 to $200. Check back soon for an official release date.






See original story from 6/5/15 below.

It appears another round of "Wheat"-themed sneakers is heading our way.

A quartet of Nike classics - the Air Max 95, Air Max 1, Air Max 90, and the Air Force 1 - in what appears to be nubuck uppers, surfaced on the 'Gram giving indication of another possible "Wheat" collection. A longtime fan-favorite, previous editions of Wheat-flavored Nikes have become amongst the most coveted models by sneakerheads. The three AM silhouettes appear to use the same beige colored midsoles while the AF1 opts for a total gum midsole/outsole reminiscent of its Mid predecessor.

According to a member of the Talking Soles forum, this collection is expected to drop sometime in the fall. No official word has been made available so stay tuned as news develops.
