Bedchambers of horror: The UK's messiest bedrooms, in pictures

1/20

A nationwide search has crowned Emma Bateman and her son Harry (above) from Street in Somerset as the winners of the UK’s messiest bedroom. Online bed retailer, Time4Sleep.co.uk, ran the competition to find the UK bedroom most in need of a clear-out after discovering that 57% of the nation describe themselves as ‘messy’.

Emma, 40, an IT worker, has won a storage bed worth up to £500 after entering a picture of her son, Harry, aged 5 and his bedroom chaos into the competition.
