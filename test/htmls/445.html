Spanish man gored to death trying to film bull run on his mobile phone

Picture: Getty
By AFP
2:20AM BST 10 Aug 2015

Man dies of his injuries after being gored in the neck from behind while trying to capture bull run on his phone

A man has died in Spain after being gored in the neck while filming a bull run through the streets with his mobile phone.

"The young man was on the path of a bull run which he was filming with his mobile phone," said Jesus Hijosa, mayor of Villaseca de la Sagra, where the accident occurred.

"A bull surprised him from behind and gored him in the neck," he told AFP.

The incident took place in Villaseca de la Sagra, one of many towns across Spain that host annual festivals where crowds of people run ahead of a herd of bulls.

Another young man was killed during a 2010 bull run in the town.

The 32-year-old man who died on Sunday was from a neighbouring village and had come with friends to Villaseca de la Sagra on Saturday for the run, the mayor said.

"This sends shivers down our spine... we organise these events as a tradition, so that people can enjoy them, but these things happen," Mr Hijosa said.

"Bulls are dangerous animals and when there are a lot of people some don't pay attention... you have to have your wits about you."

Fifteen people have been killed in bull runs in Pamplona, the most famous and popular site of bull runs, since 1911.
