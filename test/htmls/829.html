How things got really weird for Airbnb

  • by:
  • From: news.com.au
  • July 18, 2015 1:42PM

Is Airbnb’s latest marketing campaign creepy? Source: Supplied

AIRBNB has shot into the headlines this week, for all the wrong reasons.

The accommodation rental site posted a series of tweets that left some users rather creeped out. They suggested that, as you stay in other people’s homes, you should “look through their windows” and “sleep in their beds”.

The reaction to the tweets managed to foreshadow the later release of Airbnb’s advertising video, turning this seemingly innocent TV spot into some sort of thriller.

It all started with this awkwardly phrased tweet. Then it got weirder — let's just say that the full list of their tweets will send a slight shiver down the spine.
