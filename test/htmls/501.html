Miikka Skaffari, Getty

Hey, Alex —can we borrow that dress sometime?

Alexander Skarsgård debuted a different look on the red carpet for his new movie Diary Of A Teenage Girl — one that involved a golden gown, a mile-high blonde wig and an impressive set of false eyelashes.

Diary Of A Teenage Girl is set in the 1970s, so instead of sporting a wide tie or huge mustache, Skarsgård channeled his ’70s nostalgia by opting for full retro drag.

Though Skarsgård does rock a mean ‘stache in Diary Of A Teenage Girl:

Alexander Skarsgard in “The Diary of a Teenage Girl.” (Sam Emerson/Sony Pictures Classics via AP)

His costar Bel Powley and director Marielle Heller also sported vintage looks, though we have to say, Skarsgård’s is our favorite.
