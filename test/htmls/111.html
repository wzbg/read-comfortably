Israeli Artist Draws Amazing Make-Up Art On Her Own Eyelids

Published 3 weeks ago
Like Demilked on Facebook:

Eyes are beautiful and infinitely characterful, and they are central to Tal Peleg’s makeup art. She draws miniature images on the eyelids. Tiny flowers, cartoon characters, cats, even Picasso paintings: her subjects are many and varied, and extremely detailed for their size. And we can’t even draw that well on canvas.

“Inspiration is all around me, and I give my own unique artistic interpretation using makeup,” Peleg wrote on Bored Panda. “It can be inspired by emotions, movies, fairytales, animals, food, important social matter and more.” This Israeli artist uses her own eyes, eyeshadows and eyeliners to create this fantastic art.
