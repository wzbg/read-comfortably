    McDonald’s to Open Luxury Pop-up Restaurant in Tokyo for Just One Night

    Shares
    Share Tweet Email Reddit Flipboard WhatsApp Comments
    Fine dining and Mickey D’s is hardly something you’d normally utter in a single sentence. Perhaps on par to associating H&M with haute couture or Kanye West with modesty. But alas, life can throw us many curve balls. In a recent game changer, the mammoth fast food chain has decided to open up a “special pop-up eatery” in Tokyo later this month. Dubbed Restaurant M, the effort was conceived as an attempt to “boost [McDonalds’] image in Japan.”

    It gets better. The restaurant will offer 10 groups of 20 lucky winners to enjoy the brand’s new summer range of Fresh Mac burgers which will include the Bacon Lettuce Burger, Chicken Fillet Burger and Fillet-O-Fish, each served in silverware, ceramics and glass. Highlights on the menu will include a “fresh” Mac burger served as a main course, a gelée made with fresh Mac vegetables and a French fry vichyssoise and an Oreo McFlurry with a topping of berries and served in a glass bowl.

    This divine dining experience will happen for one night only on July 27 at Roppongi Hills in Tokyo, Japan. For those unable to attend, find out how to take advantage of McDonald’s “Secret Menu.”
