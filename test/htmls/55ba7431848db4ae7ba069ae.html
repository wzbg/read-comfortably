This Hello Kitty-themed breakfast is a nervous kind of happiness

1.4k
Shares
Share
Tweet
Share
What's This?
By Sarah Spigelman Richter2015-07-28 23:52:40 UTC

Calling all fans of the wonderful and wacky world of Sanrio: Your dream breakfast is here. That is, if your dream is to eat Hello Kitty.

The Hello-Kitty themed Keio Plaza Hotel Tokyo in the Shinjuku neighborhood of Tokyo is not stopping at kitty-themed rooms. It has introduced a Hello Kitty breakfast, too.

See also: 27 People Who Can't Handle Hello Kitty Not Being a Cat

Along with your freshly squeezed orange juice, Hello Kitty croutons and Hello Kitty carrot coins, you can chow down on an omelette branded with Hello Kitty's face or a sausage and vegetable sandwich with the bread painstakingly shaped in the image of Ms. Kitty herself. All, of course, on a Hello Kitty tablecloth.

The hotel is already famous for its Hello Kitty-themed hotel rooms, including a Princess Kitty room with high heel chairs, Hello Kitty water bottles and pink bows everywhere.

Suffice to say, if you build Hello Kitty, they (everyone in the Hello Kitty loving free world) will come.
