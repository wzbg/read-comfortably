Online forecasts indicate that tomorrow will be cloudy in the morning, followed by flash floods in the afternoon, but how do we really know what that looks like? Well, Ken Kawamoto, an ingenious young engineer, created Tempescope, a physical box display that reproduces tomorrow’s forecast under your own weather-proof roof. Don’t feel like worrying about what to wear tomorrow or whether you’re likely to get drenched? Tempescope’s got your back. This little box can visualize and reproduce real raindrops, clouds, simulated lightning and sunlight, so you can make sure you’re prepared!
