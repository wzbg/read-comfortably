Check Out These Creepy & Beautiful Abandoned Amusement Parks

Design News
Pin it

Ever seen a roller coaster overgrown with vegetation? In his latest book, Bizarro: The World's Most Hauntingly Beautiful Abandoned Theme Parks, photographer Seph Lawless explores ten such parks across the country.

From the Wizard of Oz park in North Carolina to Funtime Amusement Park and Zoo in Indiana, Lawless' book has 200 images of these former destinations in varying degrees of disrepair.

In the past, Lawless has photographed abandoned malls and America's Rust Belt, seeking out the places and things people have left behind.
