15 Illustrated Truths About Cats That Every Owner Will Understand

Published 2 weeks ago
Like Demilked on Facebook:

Cats are awesome, the whole internet agrees – those who don’t are driven out by angry mobs – and Lingvistov is here again to provide us with some Cat Truths. From the feline ability to appear like they’re nobility to somewhat bizarre seating choices, it touches on many points of cat-hood and cat ownership. Celebrate your furry friends with these cute illustrations!

Lingvistov is a group of like minded English language students in Russia who discovered their mutual of love of both grammar and drawing (mostly cats). They are prolific, and as such, are frequent guests on our website. They have oreviously written about sleep-lovers’ problems, cats speaking and benefits of having feline friends.
