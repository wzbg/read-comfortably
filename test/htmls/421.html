Hunt for young tech leaders at Festival of Code

27 July 2015 Last updated at 09:45 BST

It might be the school holidays for some, but for many children around the UK lessons of a different kind are just beginning.

More than 1,000 young computer enthusiasts, aged between five and 18, are taking part in the Festival of Code.

They are learning how to create websites, games, and apps.

Tim Muffett reports.
