LED Backlit Bathtub by Aquamass

Known for its beautiful bathtub designs, Brussels-based bath brand Aquamass is making waves with its LED backlit bathtub. Elvis Pompilio redesigned and reintroduced his Original Stone One concept, now new and improved, and dressed to impress. This bathtub is available in a variety of finishes including red, white, gray, black, African pigments, and even draped in strings of crystals and pearls for some extra sparkle. The LED backlighting really makes this detail shine. Complement this chic bathtub with a crystal chandelier hanging overhead, for the ultimate in bathroom luxury. For more information visit Aquamass.
