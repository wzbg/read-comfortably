Clever Illustrations That Compare the Two Types of People in This World

by Justin Page at 2:34 pm on June 10, 2015

  • Share on Tumblr
  • Email

2 Kinds of People is a Tumblr blog created by Lisbon, Portugal-based art director Joao Rocha featuring some of his clever illustrations that compare the two types of people in this world. More funny designs from his collection are available to view online.

There are only 2 kinds of people in this world, those that find this blog hilarious and those that have no sense of humor whatsoever.
