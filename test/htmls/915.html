Alexa Chung's Spot-On Fashion Advice

by: Michelle Scanga
Here’s something to think about.

  • share it

  • Tweet
  • Email
PHOTO: @chungalexa

Looks like Justin Bieber isn’t the only celebrity advocating against the word “never.” When Refinery29 asked Alexa Chung, “What cut of jean would you never wear, and why?”, the denim designer responded with the best answer: “Never say never in fashion, because you’ll be wearing ‘never’ in two years’ time.” We couldn’t agree more. Think back to mom jeans or overalls—two trends we’re sure plenty of fashion girls, at some point, claimed they’d “never” wear. Just a little something to consider the next time you are quick to judge a new trend.

Update your summer wardrobe with one of our favorite trend-driven pieces.
