Romanian Artist Creates Miniature Food Art Using Spoons As Canvases

Published 2 days ago
Like Demilked on Facebook:

Don’t play with your food, unless you’re a food artist. Better yet, an artist that makes tiny food art on spoons. That’s the canvas of choice of Ioana Vinc, a Romanian architect who likes to take a break from designing buildings by arranging tiny food items on a spoon. It’s some impressive work: looking at Kermit the (Spoon) Frog, you can hardly tell it apart from original.

Vinc’s secret? Using big spoons! Well, regular big spoons, not novelty jaw destroyers. It’s part of her passion for creating art using everyday objects. In this case, extremely small bits of everyday objects.
