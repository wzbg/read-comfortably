    Triband Plus: The Wearable Backup Battery, Charging Cable, Flash Drive, and Flashlight

    $20 USD

    Triband Plus is aimed at redefining wearable technology to provide more choices to lifestyle augmentation. It charges any android, windows or lightning compatible (iPhone 5/6) smartphones with an extra boost in power with its 800mAh lithium polymer battery and can even be used as a charging cable. Containing a flash drive, Triband can be used to view or store files on an OTG compatible smartphone (android or windows phones only) or on any PC.
