    7 Podcasts Every Fashion Girl Should Download

    In honor of Apple celebrating 10 years of podcasts this month, we rounded up the ultimate podcast guide for every fashion lover. From designers to trends and pop culture, these are the podcasts worth downloading now for a stylish listening session.

    1) MODTV Fashion Video

    For those not attending Fashion Week, this is your all-access VIP pass from backstage to the front row. Each episode features interviews, trend reports and backstage videos of top models, designers and makeup artists who create the magic for all the best runways from New York to Paris. Consider it your behind-the-scenes lens.

    2) Pop Fashion​

    ​Bringing fashion and pop culture together, vintage store owner Lisa Rowan and fashion stylist Kaarin Vembar's weekly segment discusses all the latest in fashion, celebrity and business. With a smart and creative approach to all things pop culture and fashion related, this podcast offers a fresh view to fashion's most current events.

    Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

    3) You Must Remember This

    Not directly related to strictly fashion, You Must Remember This, ​instead explores the history of 20th Century Hollywood with mini-series covering topics like Charles Manson's Hollwood, famous scandals and the actors of the Golden Age. Each in-depth episode exposes what really happened behind-the-scenes of some of Hollywood's biggest films and gossip. For the fashion girl who loves to take inspiration from the past—and know the facts to go along with it.

    4) Unbuttoned by Yahoo Style

    Tapping some of fashion's biggest names, Unbuttoned ​features guest stars for each episode with Joe Zee as host. From recapping Fashion Week and the red carpet to analyzing pop culture and playing Cards Against Fashion Humanity, there's always a fun, style-related conversation to follow along.

    5) American Fashion

    For the true fashion nerd at heart, American Fashion goes in-depth to all of the ins and outs of the fashion industry as a whole. Hosts Charles Beckwith, Seth Friedermann and Lisa-Maria Radano, focus solely on the American garment industry in conversations led by designers, retail, news, buyers, fashion technology entrepreneurs, runway photographers and more. The opinionated and educational podcast is a must-listen for anyone working in or striving to work in fashion.

    6) Chanel Fashion

    Taking it one step further than the runway, Chanel's podcast offers up exclusive behind-the-scenes videos and clips from all the latest collections. Perfect for those who are mesmerized by Lagerfeld's runway spectacles, the podcast opens up a new world of Chanel that many don't get to see.

    7) Stuff You Should Know

    Encompassing much more than just fashion, Stuff You Should Know is a useful and fascinating podcast for everyone. With topics ranging from how perfume works to an insider look at the paleo diet, every question you could ever imagine is answered in one of the many in-depth episodes.
